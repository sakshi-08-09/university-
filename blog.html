Our Certificate & Online Programs for 2023

IT Brew shares the latest news and analysis of trends shaping the IT industry, particularly within the areas of cybersecurity, big data, software development, remote work, cloud computing, and data privacy. Delivered in an easily digestible format and style, their daily newsletter is the perfect resource for all levels of IT knowledge and expertise. In the rapidly evolving world of InfoSec, Sentinel Labs is where you’ll find highly-informed deep dives into the latest cybersecurity dangers and developments. Threat researchers and vetted contributors share critical information on malware, exploits, cybercrime, and APTs, with the aim of contributing to a safer digital space for everyone. Wired is a popular tech blog that observes how technology affects human lives across culture, politics, and security.
Business leaders can access informative news, delivered through approachable, easy-to-understand content. Focused on breakthroughs and innovation, you’ll also find hot takes on modern life, tech trends, and gadget reviews.Gizmodo has been around for over two decades and is one of the best places to find expert reviews of new gadgets as they hit the market. You’ll also find insightful news on privacy, environmental policy, and labor, among other subjects. Known to serve up tech knowledge with a side of humor, it’s highly respected for its honest and transparent content.
The Verge examines how technology affects all of our lives – both now and into the future. Created for a mainstream audience, the blog presents engaging content with a straightforward approach. Find news, podcasts, feature stories, guidebooks, product reviews, and more among their resources.CNET is a long-running tech publisher that “tells you what’s new and why it matters.” Populated with news, reviews, and deep dives, the site’s content is intended to help readers make informed decisions about the tech they use and buy. In more recent years, their content has also expanded to cover wellness and financial advice.
